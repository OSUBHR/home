About Buckeyes for Harm Reduction

Our Story

Buckeyes for Harm Reduction began as a grassroots initiative by Ohio State University students who recognized the devastating impact of the opioid epidemic on their community. Driven by compassion and evidence-based public health principles, we started BHR to provide support, education, and advocacy around harm reduction.

Our Mission

Buckeyes for Harm Reduction is a grassroots student organization that aims to provide support to those affected by addiction and the opioid epidemic by advocating for harm reduction at the local, state, and national levels. We engage with the campus community to provide students with harm reduction resources and empower them to advocate for comprehensive public health reforms that prevent overdoses and increase public well-being.

What We Do

BHR takes a multifaceted approach to minimizing the risks posed by the opioid epidemic around campus and in the Greater Columbus Area. Our work includes:

Education

We educate students about the risks of opioids, dispel common misunderstandings, and teach them how to help.

Distribution

We distribute resources like Narcan and fentanyl test strips to reduce the chance of overdose and save lives.

Advocacy

We advocate for policy change at local, state, and national levels that would help those affected by the opioid crisis.

Community Service

We provide students with volunteering experiences to directly help those in need in and around Columbus.

Why We Do This

The opioid crisis is one of the most pressing public health challenges facing our country and our community. Every day, overdose takes lives. Many of these deaths are preventable. We believe that everyone deserves access to harm reduction tools, compassionate support, and evidence-based treatment. We refuse to accept a future where overdose deaths continue unchecked.

Our work is grounded in public health research, personal experiences, and a commitment to social justice. We work to reduce stigma, challenge harmful policies, and build a community that values all human life.
