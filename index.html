Our Mission

Buckeyes for Harm Reduction is a grassroots student organization that aims to provide support to those affected by addiction and the opioid epidemic by advocating for harm reduction at the local, state, and national levels. We engage with the campus community to provide students with harm reduction resources and empower them to advocate for comprehensive public health reforms that prevent overdoses and increase public well-being.

Who Are We?

Education

We educate students about the risks of opioids, dispel common misunderstandings, and teach them how to help.

Resources

We distribute resources like Narcan and fentanyl test strips to reduce the chance of overdose and save lives.

Advocacy

We advocate for policy change at local, state, and national levels to help those affected by the opioid crisis.

Community

We provide students with volunteering experiences to directly help those in need in and around Columbus.
